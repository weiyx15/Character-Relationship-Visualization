Graph Visualization

Drama Role Relation Visualization Based on Force Directed Graph

This is the final project for the course

"Information Visualization & Visual Analysis"

There are three members in our group. They are Yuxuan Wei, Yufeng Chen, Yuyuan Kang. This project is used to visualize role relation in a given drama. It visualizes the relation based on force-directed graph. There are two main functions, one for small graph allowing using time slider to find out relation changes as the story goes, one for big graph allowing expanding and contract details.

Click the left button to explore function one, right button to function two.
